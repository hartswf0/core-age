The Three Tools
This system works like building with LEGO:

SWISS - Pick and build individual parts (minifigs, vehicles, buildings)
FRANK - Arrange them on a grid (like a LEGO baseplate)
GRACE - See the full 3D scene (the finished model)

Each tool does ONE job, and they all talk to each other automatically.

Build Your First Minifig
Where: Open SWISS (click the first dot at the top)

What to do:
1. Click the yellow dot to open MINIFIGURATOR
2. Pick any hat, head, torso, and legs from the lists
3. Click "SAVE MPD" at the bottom

What happens:
Your minifig is saved and sent to FRANK automatically via the "wag-frank" bus. You'll see a confirmation message.
BEHIND THE SCENES

  kind: 'minifig-mpd',
  payload: { name, mpdLines }

Place on the Grid
Where: Switch to FRANK tab at the top

What to do:
1. Look in the left panel - your minifig is in the INBOX
2. Click on your minifig to select it (it highlights)
3. Click any grid cell (like D4 or F6)
4. Click "Assign to Cell" button

What happens:
Your minifig snaps to that grid position. The cell turns gold to show it's occupied. You can place multiple minifigs by repeating these steps.
üí° TIP: The 9√ó9 grid is like a LEGO baseplate - each cell is 160 studs apart

View in 3D
Where: Still in FRANK, then GRACE opens

What to do:
1. In FRANK, click "Build Scene in Grace"
2. GRACE opens automatically (or switch to GRACE tab)
3. Your scene loads in full 3D!

What happens:
FRANK composes an MPD file with all your grid positions, sends it to GRACE via the bus, and GRACE renders it as a 3D scene. You can orbit, zoom, and inspect everything.
